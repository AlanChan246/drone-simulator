# Kenney Starter Kit City Builder assets

Source: https://github.com/KenneyNL/Starter-Kit-City-Builder

The 3D models used by Mission 1 are CC0 assets from Kenney's Starter Kit City Builder. The simulator reimplements the grid-based city composition in Three.js; it does not include or execute Godot/GDScript code.

`LICENSE.md` and `UPSTREAM-README.md` are retained alongside the assets for attribution and reference.
